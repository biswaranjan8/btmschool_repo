Professional Teachers

According to an adage “Teachers touch eternity; they never know where their influence may end.”BTM has professionally qualified and experienced teachers who undergo BTM induction program to adapt themselves with innovative and creative methods of teaching. Teachers in a quest to gain more knowledge have taken up many courses affiliated to Trinity of London and actively participated in many subject workshops in Hyderabad and other cities. They constantly provide benchmarks to the students for improvement and guide them to success and elevating us to the status of one of the best Elementary Schools in OSDISHA.

Latest Events

BTM School has become synonymous in introducing new events to supplement children’s academic curriculum. Change has become an integral part of life and to meet this demand BTM has introduced many programmes like organizing Carnivals, Graduation day, farewell, science exhibition, Robotics, and Stem Labs to excite and stimulate the students thinking and learning capacity.
